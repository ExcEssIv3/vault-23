## Shared daily rhythm

Most residents follow the same daily shape, adjusted around their specific work/school block:

- 06:00–07:00 — Wake, breakfast (dining hall, Level 2)
- 07:00–12:00 — Morning work/school block
- 12:00–13:00 — Lunch (dining hall)
- 13:00–18:00 — Afternoon work/school block
- 18:00–19:00 — Dinner (dining hall)
- 19:00–22:00 — Free time (atrium, bar/celebration room, or own housing unit)
- 22:00–06:00 — Sleep (own housing unit)

Individual character files only call out where this shared rhythm applies to them and where they deviate from it (specific workplace, unusual hours, days off).

## Week structure

Monday–Friday are work/school days; Saturday–Sunday are rest days. Exceptions:

- **Enforcement** (Walter, Edgar, Thomas) covers day/swing/night shifts seven days a week — see their files for the rotation.
- **Silas** keeps the clinic open on a reduced basis on weekends, since pregnancies don't observe one.
- **Caleb** keeps a reduced monitoring shift on weekends — water treatment can't be left fully unattended.
- **Amos** is off on weekends barring emergencies (e.g. the reactor leak).
- **George IX**, as Overseer, is effectively always on call, though he keeps lighter office hours on weekends.
- **Clyde** has two entirely separate schedules depending on whether his §3.1 thread in [story-beats.md](../story-beats.md) resolves — see his file.

## Children

All 14 named children attend the Classroom (Level 2, Prudence's room) Monday–Friday during the standard school block, and are off on weekends. Individual child files note only their parents and housing, not a repeated schedule.
